Fake News Analyzer

NLP & AI Project Case Study
#NLP #Media #FakeNews

Project Overview

This project focuses on combating misinformation by using state-of-the-art natural language processing (NLP) and large language models (LLMs) for fake news detection and classification. The system is designed to help news organizations and platforms maintain information integrity and public trust.

Problem

The rapid spread of fake news and disinformation poses a threat to democracy, public opinion, and social trust. Traditional detection methods struggle with the subtlety and scale of modern misinformation campaigns, requiring more advanced, automated solutions.

Solution

The solution leverages advanced AI models, including fine-tuned GPT-4 Omni and BERT, to classify news articles as real or fake. The system uses few-shot learning and contextual analysis to capture subtle relationships in text, significantly outperforming traditional models like CNNs in accuracy and robustness.

Results

  • Accuracy: 98.6% (fine-tuned GPT-4 Omni model)
  • Impact: Automated, scalable fake news detection for media organizations
  • Industry Significance: Enhanced transparency and integrity in journalism

Technologies Used

  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Large Language Models: GPT-4 Omni, BERT
  • Deep Learning: Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs)
  • Few-Shot Learning, Model Fine-Tuning
