AI Chatbot for Customer Support

Conversational AI Project Case Study
#Chatbots #CustomerSupport #NLP

Project Overview

This project delivers an AI-powered chatbot for customer support, capable of handling both text and voice queries. It automates real-time customer interactions, reduces response times, and enhances user satisfaction for businesses across industries.

Problem

Traditional customer support is resource-intensive and often slow to respond, leading to customer frustration. There was a need for a scalable, always-available solution to handle high query volumes efficiently and deliver consistent support quality.

Solution

The solution uses advanced NLP and speech recognition to understand and respond to customer queries in natural language. The chatbot integrates with CRM systems, supports multi-channel deployment (web, mobile, messaging apps), and provides analytics for continuous improvement.

Results

  • Efficiency: Automated 70%+ of customer queries, reducing average response time by 60%
  • Customer Satisfaction: Improved CSAT scores and 24/7 support availability
  • Industry Use: Deployed in e-commerce, banking, and telecom sectors

Technologies Used

  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Speech Recognition
  • Dialog Management & Analytics
  • Python, Rasa, Dialogflow, Google Speech-to-Text
