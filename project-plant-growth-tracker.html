AI-Based Plant Growth Tracker

Computer Vision & Agriculture Project Case Study
#AgriTech #ComputerVision #SmartFarming

Project Overview

This project demonstrates how artificial intelligence and computer vision are revolutionizing agriculture by enabling automated plant growth tracking, disease detection, and yield prediction. AI-driven systems help farmers optimize crop health and resource use for sustainable farming.

Problem

Traditional crop monitoring relies on manual observation, which is time-consuming and prone to error. Farmers need accurate, real-time insights into soil, weather, and crop health to maximize yield and minimize loss from disease or pests.

Solution

AI-powered systems integrate IoT sensors, drones, and computer vision models to monitor soil conditions, detect crop diseases, and track plant growth stages. Algorithms analyze images and sensor data to automatically adjust irrigation, nutrients, and pest management, ensuring optimal growth conditions.

Results

  • Yield Increase: Up to 30% higher crop yields (University of California, Davis study)
  • Resource Efficiency: Significant reduction in water and fertilizer usage
  • Disease Detection: Over 90% accuracy in detecting crop diseases and pests

Technologies Used

  • IoT Sensors & Drones
  • Computer Vision & Deep Learning
  • Data Analytics & Predictive Modeling
