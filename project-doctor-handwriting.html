Doctor Handwriting Recognition

AI & OCR Project Case Study
#Healthcare #OCR #AI

Project Overview

This project addresses the challenge of digitizing handwritten medical prescriptions and doctor notes using artificial intelligence and optical character recognition (OCR) technologies. The goal is to improve patient safety and streamline healthcare workflows by accurately interpreting complex handwriting.

Problem

Illegible handwritten prescriptions can lead to medication errors and delays in patient care. Manual interpretation by pharmacists is time-consuming and error-prone. A robust, automated solution was needed to reliably convert handwritten notes into digital text.

Solution

The system uses deep learning techniques—particularly Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) and Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs)—to recognize and digitize handwritten text from images of prescriptions and notes. The model is trained on diverse handwriting samples to handle variations in writing style and complexity.

Results

  • Accuracy: State-of-the-art deep learning models achieve high accuracy on real-world prescription data
  • Impact: Reduced medication errors, faster processing, and improved patient safety
  • Industry Significance: Enables digital health records and automation in healthcare

Technologies Used

  • Optical Character Recognition (OCR)
  • Deep Learning: CNNs, RNNs
  • Image Processing & Data Augmentation
